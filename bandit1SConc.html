Outside The Bandit Lair

He lets out a strained gurgle and spits blood which you enjoy probably more than is right and god fearing. With the first one dispatched you turn your attention to the two remaining brigands in the cottage. Will you…

  • Call them out, alerting them to your presence and fight them outside
  • Run away
