Outside The Bandit Lair

You charge the bandit by the fire. Having become aware of you, he stands ready, weapon in hand, but you see a look of fear in his eyes.

You clumsily stab him repeatedly with the pointy end of your pitch fork. He falls to the ground, your frenzied rage was his demise. You feel a pain in your side and notice a dagger in your ribs; you remove it and blood flows from the wound.

The first bandit is dead but you hear a stirring from the cottage and the remaining 2 brigands are coming to face you, ready for battle.

  • Run away
