Searching the forrest

You trudge and falter through the forest in search of the bandit’s lair. You are aware wasting precious time and energy in your efforts, and you wish you had a more precise location to work with. Eventually you smell smoke and hear a fire and stumble on what you think is the bandits camp, unfortunately, you made a lot of noise on your approach and you hear a loud voice shout “who goes there!” Will you…

  • Emerge from the trees and face him.
  • Return to the village.
  • Run away.
